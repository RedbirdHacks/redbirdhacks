What is RedbirdHacks?

RedbirdHacks is ISU's first university hackathon for undergraduates to prototype, program, and pitch their ideas to corporate sponsors in an entrepreneurial fashion, connecting Arts Technology, Information Technology, and Business students to cultivate innovation and collaboration to empower all students.
